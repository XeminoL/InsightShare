### Main content
A monthly community event where speakers from a few companies shared how they build and run AI on AWS. Anh Steve Tran from Cloud Thinker opened with his own career path and an agentic platform that assists cloud engineers. Anh Nghi, anh Kiet and anh Trung from Renova Cloud and RhoAI talked about a Vietnamese voice assistant and why they route it through text instead of speech-to-speech. Chi Bao and anh Nguyen from Cloud Kinetic showed a DevOps agent that helps investigate incidents. Anh Truong and chi Minh Anh from Noventiq walked through using Amazon Quick for HR work such as CV screening, and anh Toan closed with how to connect Amazon Quick to a service privately instead of over the public internet. Most talks ended with a short demo.

### What I learned
The point that stuck with me, repeated across the talks, was that these AI tools assist engineers rather than replace them: they suggest or draft, and a person still decides. That is close to how my own project works, where the AI analyzes and answers but the user stays in control. I also picked up something from anh Toan's talk that I had not thought about, that connecting an AI to a service is not the whole job in a company, keeping that connection off the public internet matters just as much. A few of the speakers work in solution and sales roles rather than pure engineering, which gave me a wider view of where cloud knowledge can lead.
