### Main content
A monthly community event with speakers from a few companies sharing how they build and run AI on AWS. The talks covered agentic tools that assist cloud engineers, a Vietnamese voice assistant, an agent that helps investigate incidents, and using Amazon Quick to speed up HR work like CV screening. Most talks ended with a short demo.

### What I learned
The point that stuck with me was that these AI tools assist engineers rather than replace them: they suggest or draft, and a person still decides. That is close to how my own project works, where the AI analyzes and answers but the user stays in control. A few speakers also work in solution and sales roles rather than pure engineering, which gave me a wider view of where cloud knowledge can lead.
